# Amazon-Sales-Analysis-Project
End-to-end data analytics project using an e-commerce dataset inspired by Amazon. Includes Excel cleaning, SQL analysis, and a Power BI dashboard with DAX KPIs for Sales, Profit, Growth, and market insights. Demonstrates real business intelligence and data storytelling skills.
🚀 SQL & Power BI Project – Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard
Excited to share my latest data analytics project where I analyzed an Amazon Sales Dataset using Excel, SQL & Power BI to uncover business insights and growth opportunities.

📊 Key Highlights from the Dashboard.

🔹 Analyzed 20K+ orders generating $18.29M in sales and $1.21M profit
🔹 Achieved 24.98% sales growth with a 6.6% profit margin
🔹 Built interactive KPI tracking for Sales, Profit, Orders & Growth using DAX
🌍 Market Insights • 82% revenue comes from the United States, indicating high market dependency
• India shows strong growth potential for expansion
• Identified revenue concentration risk and diversification opportunities.

💳 Customer Behavior

• Credit cards dominate as the preferred payment method
• Strong adoption of digital payments (UPI & Amazon Pay)
• Minimal Cash-on-Delivery usage indicates high trust in online shopping.

🛍 Product & Category Insights

• Top products: Puzzle 1000 pcs, Memory Card, Instant Pot, Kids Toy Car
• Electronics & Home categories drive the highest revenue
• Profit is evenly distributed across categories → lower business risk.

📈 Business Recommendations.

✔ Expand marketing in emerging markets like India
✔ Optimize margins via supplier negotiation & cost control
✔ Run seasonal campaigns and bundle offers for top products
This project helped me strengthen my skills in data cleaning, SQL analysis, DAX calculations, and dashboard storytelling.
